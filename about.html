My Name is Delmar Taylor

I'm Delmar Taylor and I have a keen interest in helping others via fitness/nutrition and sports training. I was born and raised in Nassau, Bahamas and came to the United States during my early teen years. Upon my arrival into the U.S. I played American football in high school earning a full athletic scholarship to study and play football at Florida International University. After graduating with my bachelor's degree I quickly realized fitness/sports training was something I wanted to pursue. I was passionate about it from day 1! I stayed in this field as I pursued employment at U.S.1 Fitness Center, a gym located in North Miami, Fl. Once I started working at the gym I told my employers that I wanted to be a trainer. They supported my goals fully and helped me obtain my personal training certification through ISSA. The rest is history!
